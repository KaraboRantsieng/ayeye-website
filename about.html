Who We Are

A studio that bets on you

AYEYE Creative Agency was founded with one conviction: small businesses deserve great digital products — not watered-down, budget-compromised versions of them.

We serve entrepreneurs and business owners across Johannesburg, Gauteng and beyond. Our clients are café owners, event planners, caterers, retailers, consultants — people building real things, who need a real digital partner.

Our model is simple and unusual: we build your website or app first. We do the work. We take on the risk. You review the finished product and only pay once you're completely satisfied. If you're not happy, you owe us nothing.

This isn't a gimmick — it's how we hold ourselves accountable and how we earn your trust from day one.
